Odd Animals

We here at Odd Animals know that, without human awareness and love, the survival of many non-human species is threatened. To this end, we aim to bring as many unique, quirky, strangely-beautiful, and downright weird animals from every corner of this world to your screen. We know that your viewing pleasure will lead to love - and your love will lead to action, protecting the amazing creatures that share this earth with humankind.
